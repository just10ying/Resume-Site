Software Developer

Hello!

I'm Justin, a senior computer engineering major from West Chester, Pennsylvania. At the University of Pittsburgh, I've been active on campus as part of the board of the Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers (SASE) chapter at Pitt, where I served as a web administrator, maintained membership records, and coordinated events with national SASE. Outside of school and work, I enjoy making music with friends; I've played in several ensembles (Pitt's symphony orchestra and UMD's Gamer Symphony Orchestra) and have received numerous recognitions, including an invitation to play in Carnegie Hall. When I'm not practicing violin, you can find me assembling models or working on personal coding projects. I've volunteered as a freelance programmer for my local church, developing an automated system that generates and formats records for tax reporting, and I've also worked closely with mechanical engineers at Pitt to create a software tool for determining the effects of radiation heat transfer in thermoelectric devices.

Nice to meet you!
